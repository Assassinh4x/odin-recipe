Simple Deviled Eggs

Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are a perfect appetizer. This easy deviled eggs reciped is delicious,and its simple to make more for larger gatherings.Onion and celery were added for a little more flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks;set whites aside.
  2. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, onion, and celery; mix well.
  3. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika. Regrigerate until serving.
